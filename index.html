Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries about the democratic process.

Q: What if I lose my Voter ID card?

A: You can apply for a duplicate Voter ID card online. You can also vote using alternative approved photo IDs (like a passport, driving license, or PAN card) as long as your name is on the electoral roll.

Q: Can I vote from another city?

A: Usually, you must vote in the constituency where you are registered. If you moved, you must update your address to register in your new constituency.

Q: Is voting mandatory?

A: While highly encouraged as a civic duty, voting is not legally mandatory in most contexts.
